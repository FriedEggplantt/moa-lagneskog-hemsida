This is an informative League of Legends site, using it is simple! Go to the home page, select your preffered category, select your champion, and there is all the information you need to get started!

The information is suitable for both beginners that wants to know how to prepare for you game and in-game items, but also for intermediate players who can quickly go in and find the right runes and build for a their chosen champion.

The site features champions from the in-game region Noxus, which has a fearsome reputation, for it is viewed as brutal and threatening. Meanwhile inside you find an inclusive society where the strength and talents are highly respected.

The noxians have a wild range of categories, from mages to assassins to fighters and more, with champions in each category. The next time you open the game, try one of them!
